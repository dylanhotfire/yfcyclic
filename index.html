How does Cyclic work?

  • Dividends

    Right now many other yearn finance protocols have one issue: THE TOKEN HAS NO INHERENT VALUE! Tokens from other yearn forks are only used for governance which is nice, but the majority of token holders may not even take part in governance decisions. This is why Cyclic gives value to the token holders not just through governance, but also through DIVIDENDS! Because Cyclic is cyclical in nature, it can reward all who participate in its ecosystem. Farmers are incredibly important to yearn ecosystems, but that doesnt mean they should be the only ones who are rewarded for participating. If a farmer participates in stable coin vaults as well as holds the tokens they earned, the combined dividends and stable coin APY will mean a hefty ROI week after week.

  • Cyclical

    Other yearn finance forks currently struggle to find the right balance between yield returned, yield locked, and value for the token holder. Many of them simply ignore token value altogether! When the APY of stable coin vaults is dependent on token value, this is a huge oversight and cuts out potential for everyone participating in the ecosystem. Cyclic fixes this with a weekly cycling profit structure. Right now there are three possible cycles the vaults can be in: Gold (for the farmers), Green (for the token holders), and Purple (For permanent Autonomous Money Pile value). During a Gold vault cycle the goal of the vaults is to accrue as high of TVL as possible. During a Green vault cycle the goal of the vaults is to provide token holders with dividends they can claim and hopefully deter farmers from selling their hard-earned tokens. Last but not least, the goal of a Purple vault cycle is to take advantage of a stable ecosystem and collect permanent AMP value.

  • Forever

    Yes, we mean forever. As long as Ethereum is running and stable coins can be staked somewhere like yearn.finance this protocol will compound and reward dividends. Even after emissions have ended and the token supply is completely in the hands of the community it will still reward those who hold the token. Dividends can sit in the contract forever and act like a yearly pay check or be collected weekly to pay for your daily coffee, its up to the user to claim their dividends and all it takes is one simple transaction. Because the Autonomous Money Pile is owned by the contract and guided by community governance the money held in the contract remains decentralized and compounding, forever. In addition, because of the modular setup of the Cyclic vaults, governance decisons are easily implemented.
